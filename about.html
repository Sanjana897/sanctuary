About the project

Jesús crossed the border. Rosa survived Auschwitz. Bertha left the Deep South. Teddy was born in an internment camp. Henry feeds the homeless. Nerdeen fights for her homeland. Follow the journeys of these six individuals who have found—or are continuing to seek out—sanctuary in New York City.

Finding Sanctuary is a multimedia project designed and produced by the graduate students in New York University’s Studio 20 program, and friends from the NYU Journalism and Interactive Telecommunications programs. Discover additional stories by following us on Facebook, Instagram and Twitter.
